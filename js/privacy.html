1 Data We Collect

BasketMate collects only the minimum data required to provide the service. No account registration or email address is required.

Data Purpose Where Stored
Display name (e.g. "Andreas") Show who added items to the shared list Device only (localStorage)
Household code Link family members to the same list Device + our database
Shopping list data (items, aisles, stores) Provide the core app functionality Our database
Push notification token Send shopping alerts to your household Our database
Language preference Display the app in your chosen language Device only (localStorage)
Purchase token (premium users only) Verify in-app purchase via Google Play Our database

2 Data We Don't Collect

  • Real name, email address, or phone number
  • Location or GPS data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Browsing history or behavioural analytics
  • Payment card details (handled entirely by Google Play)
  • Photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Contacts or address book
🔒 We do not use any third-party analytics SDKs, advertising trackers, or crash reporting tools.

3 How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide and improve the BasketMate service:

  • Syncing your shopping list in real time across household members
  • Showing who added each item to the shared list
  • Sending push notifications when a family member adds an item (premium only, while shopping mode is open)
  • Saving your aisle layout, favourite products and store preferences
  • Verifying your in-app purchase to unlock premium features for your household

We do not use your data for marketing, profiling, or any purpose other than delivering the app's features.

4 Payments & Billing

BasketMate offers an optional one-time in-app purchase ("BasketMate Family — £2.99") processed exclusively through Google Play Billing.

  • All payment transactions are handled by Google LLC
  • We never see, store, or process your payment card details
  • We only receive a purchase token from Google to verify the purchase was legitimate
  • Google's privacy policy applies to the payment process: policies.google.com/privacy
⚠️ Refund requests for in-app purchases must be made through the Google Play Store, not through AtStudios directly.

5 Push Notifications

If you grant notification permission, we store a push subscription token in our database linked to your household ID. This token is used only to notify other household members when an item is added to the shopping list, and only when they have the shopping list open.

  • You can revoke notification permission at any time in your device settings or browser settings
  • Revoking permission automatically disables notifications — your token is removed from our system within 24 hours of the next failed delivery attempt
  • Push notifications are a premium feature and are not available on the free plan

7 Data Storage & Security

Your shopping data is stored on Neon PostgreSQL servers located in us-east-1 (United States). Data transferred between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.2+.

⚠️ Your household code is the only key to your shopping list. Anyone with this code can view and edit your list. Keep it private and only share it with trusted family members.

We implement the following security measures:

  • All database connections use SSL/TLS encryption
  • Household data is isolated — users can only access their own household's data
  • No passwords are stored — authentication is code-based only
  • Push subscription tokens are stored securely and used only for their intended purpose

8 Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you actively use the app. Specifically:

  • Shopping list data — retained indefinitely while the household exists
  • Push tokens — automatically removed within 24 hours if delivery fails (e.g. app uninstalled)
  • Purchase tokens — retained indefinitely to maintain premium status
  • Local data (name, language, preferences) — stored on your device until you clear app data or uninstall

If you request deletion of your household data, we will remove it within 30 days. See Section 11 for how to request deletion.

9 Children's Privacy

BasketMate is a household shopping tool designed for family use. It is not directed at children under 13 as the primary user. The app does not require any personal information to function — only a display name of the user's choice.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through our service, please contact us at atouliatos45@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

11 Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your BasketMate household data at any time. This will permanently remove:

  • Your household and all associated shopping lists
  • All aisles, products, and favourites
  • All push notification subscriptions linked to your household
  • Your purchase record (note: this will not trigger a Google Play refund)
⚠️ Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All family members using your household code will lose access to the shared list.

To request deletion, email us at atouliatos45@gmail.com with the subject line "BasketMate Data Deletion Request" and include your 6-character household code. We will process your request within 30 days.
